Achievements

As a dedicated member of the Research and Development Department at AVN Institute of Engineering & Technology, I collaborated with a dynamic interdisciplinary team comprising coordinators, ECE students, and peers from AIML and CSE to conceptualize and develop an AI-driven Rover project over the span of eight months. This endeavor involved rigorous coding, integration of embedded systems, and implementation of machine learning algorithms to enhance autonomous navigation and real-time data processing capabilities. Our team meticulously optimized the Rover’s hardware-software synergy, tackling challenges such as sensor calibration, path planning, and adaptive decision-making under constrained environments.
The project culminated in our participation at Rudrasandhan 2K25, a national-level technical fest hosted by our institute, where we showcased the Rover’s innovative applications in disaster response and terrain mapping. Competing against top-tier institutions, our solution earned the First Position in the Project Expo held on 6th–7th March 2025. This achievement not only reflects our technical prowess but also underscores the power of cross-departmental collaboration, blending expertise from robotics, AI/ML, and software engineering.
Recognized by faculty and industry judges for its scalability and real-world relevance, this project has been a cornerstone of my portfolio, demonstrating my ability to lead complex technical initiatives, adapt to evolving challenges, and deliver impactful solutions. I am proud to contribute to AVN’s legacy of innovation and look forward to leveraging this experience in future endeavors at the intersection of AI and robotics.
